Heal's - The home of modern, designer and contemporary furniture, home accessories, lighting, garden, kitchen equipment, dining, bedroom and bathroom accessories and gifts. Beginning as a bed and mattress making firm in 1810, Heal's has been designing and making high quality furniture ever since. We have a tradition of working with the most talented designers, craftsmen and brands of the day to make furniture that is comfortable, beautiful and affordable.
