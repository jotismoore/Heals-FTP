How to redeem your Promotional Voucher

  • Go through the checkout process until you get to the final order summary page.
  • Click on the 'Promotional Voucher' button and copy the gift certificate claim code exactly as shown above.

  • If the value of your order is lower than the value of the gift certificate, the balance will be added to your account with , and you can use it on future visits.
